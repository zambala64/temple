測試用護持計畫：五尊護法
Plan: Five Dharma Protectors

緣起：堪祖透美仁波切，因建設佛學院，護法殿需要安置護法尊，因此特地至尼泊爾客製專屬的寧瑪派護法。為了利益眾生，護法尊在佛寺裡，僧眾會每天修持儀軌，護持的功德主也將獲得護法神力庇佑。
護法神尊存在佛寺多久，加持一直存在，
不論來世，功德依舊。

Origin: Because of the construction of the Buddhist Academy, the Dharma Protector Hall needs Dharma protectors, so H.H. Khentrul Thokmeth Rinpoche specially went to Nepal to customize the Nyingma Dharma protector statues. In order to benefit all beings, the Dharma Protector is in the Buddhist temple, the monks will practice rituals every day, and those who donate will also be blessed by divine power.

The custom-made statues of the Dharma protectors from Nepal cost NT$150,000 each. They are all made according to ancient traditions. After the clay is moulded, the molds are grouted, the bronzes are polished and painted, and the faces are gilded.
